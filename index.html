What's In My Fridge?

This is a simple application that allows the user to search for a recipe with any ingredients found in your fridge. If you are not sure what to make, this is the app for you. With ease of use, the program allows you to enter in different ingredients that will help you narrow down your search. Once you have listed your ingredients, an API call will return a list of various recipes that can be made with those ingredients. We list all necessary ingredients, servings, etc. You can also click the link and it will take you to a page with the whole meal.

View Project

Weather App

This is a simple to-do application that will allow you to find the weather in any city. Open up the application, and type in any city that you want to find the weather for. Whether you're on vacation, wanting to go for a run, wanting to play sports etc, this is the one for you. Once a city has been chosen, it will save all of your searches, until you decide to delete them. They will display the current forecast as well as the next 5 day forecast.

View Project

Day Planner

It can be very difficult to keep track of your busy days sometimes. That's why this day planner is perfect. Very user friendly, you can enter in different tasks/meetings you have throughout the day and save them here. All of your tasks will be saved, and as the day progresses, the tasks will be green if they are yet to happen. And they will be red once they have passed. You are able to save all of your inputs so you can go back and anytime and double check what time that meeting was!
